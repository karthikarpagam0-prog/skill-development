    <h1>Exploring Artificial Intelligence</h1>

    <p>
        <b>Artificial Intelligence (AI)</b> is the capability of computational systems to perform 
        <i>tasks</i> typically associated with <mark>human intelligence</mark>. 
        <ins>It includes learning, reasoning, and problem-solving.</ins>
    </p>

    <p>
        It is a <sup>field</sup> of research in computer science that develops methods for machines 
        to perceive their <small>environment</small> and maximize their <sub>chances</sub> of success.
    </p>

    <hr>

    <h3>Core AI Technologies</h3>
    <ul>
        <li>Machine Learning (ML)</li>
        <li>Natural Language Processing (NLP)</li>
        <li>Neural Networks</li>
    </ul>
